Brain Controlled Drone

A mock prototype that uses Muse headset beta waves to detect concentration and steer a Tello drone like a joystick. Ingenious+ Regional Winner.

Overview

I used beta wave frequencies from a Muse headset to detect concentration levels, then programmed those signals as joystick inputs for a Tello drone. This mock prototype explores hands-free drone control through focus.

The project received a $1,000 grant as an Ingenious+ Regional Winner.
