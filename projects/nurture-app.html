Overview

Developed an app through Technovation girls to help new mother identify post partum depression to gain proper healthcare support while acting as data base for medical professionals to gather accurate information and real numbers pertaining this issue.

While this started as a project, it quickly became something much bigger. As we spoke to mothers who have experienced postpartum depression, we realized the immense impact it has on individuals and society as a whole.

Now, this has form Nurture Biotech, where as Co-founder, programmer and Product Manager, I help expand our company's vision by meeting with advisors and mentors.

Problem

Postpartum depression is under-identified and under-supported. Mothers and clinicians lack accessible tools and accurate real-world data.

Approach

Built a Technovation app for postpartum depression identification and support, then grew the work into Nurture Biotech through conversations with mothers, advisors, and mentors.

My contribution

Co-founder, programmer, and Product Manager — building the product and expanding the company vision with advisors and mentors.

Technical details

Mobile app development · product management · maternal health screening workflows · clinical data considerations.

Impact

Grew from a Technovation project into Nurture Biotech, centered on maternal mental health support and clinical data.

Future directions

Continue expanding Nurture Biotech's vision with advisors, mentors, and healthcare partners.
