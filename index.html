  • Drone Designer at University of Calgary

    Worked with the Intelligent Dynamics Control Lab to create an efficient method for crop monitoring using IoT drone systems.

    As part of the WiseUCalgary research program at the University of Calgary, I developed a real-time crop monitoring drone system integrating IoT sensors. This project aimed to optimize crop health and water usage by tracking environmental data such as temperature, humidity, and light levels. Collaborating with a multidisciplinary team, I gained hands-on experience in sensor integration and data analysis, contributing to a low-cost solution that enhances agricultural efficiency while minimizing fieldwork. This opportunity deepened my understanding of the intersection between technology and sustainable farming practices.

  • Researcher at Vascular Training Platform

    Researched Vascular Diseases and neuroimaging techniques at VAST Platform UofC.

    I gained hands-on expertise in advanced neuroimaging techniques, including MRI scans and Transcranial Doppler Ultrasound, to assess and analyze cerebral health. Collaborating with leading experts in the field allowed me to deepen my knowledge of neuroimaging applications and vascular health. Additionally, I engaged in cognitive assessments, gaining insights into how lifestyle factors can influence dementia prevention. This experience has equipped me with the skills and knowledge necessary to contribute to future innovations in medical science.

  • Government of Alberta Youth Advisor

    Represented 1000+ students in Alberta to work closely with the Minister of Education and fellow council members to address key issues in our education system.

    I focused on enhancing the COVID reentry plan for students, particularly those in K-6, whose reading and math skills were impacted by prolonged educational isolation. I proposed a transition period dedicated to these two subjects while leveraging STEAM courses to strengthen their skills.p>
