Integrated Engineering @ UBC · Schulich Leader · based in Calgary · focused on healthcare tech & robotics

I’m an 18-year-old builder from Calgary passionate about creating positive change through technology and empathy-driven innovation. I believe the future is something we actively create, by imagining what could exist and building the pieces that are missing.

I’m constantly challenging myself to step beyond my comfort zone, learn from new perspectives, and approach problems through a curiosity and creativity lens. Through robotics, drones, neurotechnology, and intelligent systems, I’m exploring how technology can bridge the gap between engineering and medicine to create more human-centered solutions.

I’m optimistic about the future and working to built it.
